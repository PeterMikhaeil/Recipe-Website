Holiday Elixer Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp bone dust
  • 1 owl's eye
  • 1/2 tsp fairy dust
  • 1/2 cup goblin drool
  • 2 drops snake venom
  • 1 cinnamon stick (optional)

Instructions

  1. Muddle bone dust and the owl's eye in a glass or small drinking cauldron.
  2. Pour goblin drool and mix in fairy dust.
  3. Mix in drops of venom. When elixer becomes purple/blue, the elixer is finished.
  4. Decorate with a common witch ingredients or a cinnamon stick to make it more festive (optional).
